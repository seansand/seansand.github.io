Call of Cthulhu - Historical Archive

Chapter One: The Edge of Darkness

In which our heroes first meet, at the bedside of a mutual friend with a decades-old dark secret, and that friend asks for one last favor.

Chapter Two: The Haunting

In which our heroes travel to Boston to investigate a house which seems to torture those who dwell within.

Chapter Three: Beneath the Hill

In which our heroes visit a house in Providence, Rhode Island, and discover a tunnel that leads to caverns millions of years old, but still inhabited by strange beings.

Chapter Four: None More Black

In which our heroes investigate the tragic death of a student at Miskatonic University and a mysterious substance found in his dormitory room.

Chapter Five: Dead Light

In which our heroes, during a routine automobile road trip to New Hampshire, endure a severe thunderstorm, an unexpected stop, and pursuit from a nightmarish creature.

Chapter Six: The Werewolf of Coldwater Falls

In which our heroes travel to a small town in the country to investigate a missing girl, and her apparent killer who has seemingly undergone a strange transformation.

Chapter Seven: The Dark Stone

In which our heroes, while in Boston searching for a missing businessman, view some ghastly paintings, visit a speakeasy with some unsavory characters, and learn of a duo of villains, both fighting to possess a powerful artifact.

Chapter Eight: Mansion of Madness

In which our heroes conclude Chapter Seven, by first traveling to western Pennsylvania to defeat a former cult leader, and then back to Boston to destroy a gangster who has become horrifically more than human.

Chapter Nine: The Hopeful

In which our heroes investigate the past of a young Olympic hopeful, leading them to the strange coastal town of Innsmouth, Massachusetts.

Chapter Ten: The Madman

In which our heroes are asked by an old friend to look into the sudden insanity of a young woman and the unexplained disappearances of at first animals and then townspeople; and subsequently discover that the evil doings have an unexpected source.

Chapter Eleven: Freak Show

In which our heroes visit a traveling carnival, and learn that there is a large amount of interest in one particular exhibit.

Chapter Twelve: Circus of Fear

In which our heroes conclude Chapter Eleven, where a series of terrorizing events at the traveling carnival culminate in a house fire, two murders, and the disappearance of the infant creature all the parties were trying to obtain.

Chapter Thirteen: The Inventor

In which our heroes locate a missing physicist, who is just about to initiate a most dangerous experiment.

Chapter Fourteen: The Night Door

In which our heroes gain two unusual and unexpected allies while investigating the cause of a series of forest fires in southern Vermont.

Chapter Fifteen: The Idol of Thoth

In which our heroes prevent a horrific event from occurring at the premiere of a Boston museum exhibition.

Chapter Sixteen: The Lightless Beacon

In which our heroes, fleeing a shipwreck, find themselves on a small offshore island but terrifyingly not alone.

Chapter Seventeen: Suffer Little Children

In which our heroes team up with a New York spiritualist and look into the haunting of a wealthy philanthropist's rural New Hampshire childhood home.

Chapter Eighteen: Black Hills, Blackest Secrets

In which our heroes travel halfway across the country to look into a series of mysterious suicides at a South Dakota mining facility.

Chapter Nineteen: The Dreamer

In which our heroes go to New York City to investigate the disappearance of their spiritualist friend Paul LeMond, and learn about a strange period in his personal history.

Chapter Twenty: London Calling

In which our heroes travel across the Atlantic Ocean to London, England and uncover a plot manipulating two extremist political groups, and disrupt a dire threat against Parliament.

Chapter Twenty-one: The Thing in the Well

In which our heroes prevent a child-murder rampage in Boston, and while doing so, learn of an ancient organization known as "The Brotherhood" led by a mysterious figure who once resided (and perhaps still resides) in Transylvania.

Chapter Twenty-two: Castle Dark

In which our heroes investigate the Transylvanian castle home of the founder of the Brotherhood of the Beast, meeting the mysterious Baron in a brief but deadly confrontation.

Chapter Twenty-three: The Sands of Time

In which our heroes travel by camel into the wastes of the Western Desert of Egypt in search of the lost tomb of a long-dead priest, to find out more about his mysterious prophecy of the coming of "the Beast".

Chapter Twenty-four: The Halls of Celaeno

In which our heroes travel four hundred thirty light-years to a planet orbiting a star in the Pleiades, in a desperate attempt to obtain both information and a potentially useful tool in their defense against an imminent but unknown threat.

Chapter Twenty-five: Mountains of the Moon

In which our heroes look into the source of frequent earthquakes in the high mountains of Peru, and learn that a corporation they've dealt with in the past might be behind them, and for nefarious reasons.

Chapter Twenty-six: By the Bay

In which our heroes discover a cult of Deep One hybrids living in Chinatown, San Francisco, led by a mysterious figure with ties to the Brotherhood, and learn of their suspicious plot set to occur on the upcoming autumnal equinox.

Chapter Twenty-seven: Making the Future Today

In which our heroes fend off a surprise attack in the dead of night by an otherworldly monster, and trace the being's origin to an NWI science facility on the other side of the bay in Oakland.

Chapter Twenty-eight: Day of the Beast

In which our heroes return to Egypt to attempt to prevent the long-awaited summoning of the Beast.

Chapter Twenty-nine: Genius Loci

In which our heroes are relieved to finally return to their homes in Massachusetts after thwarting the plans of the Brotherhood, but must shortly thereafter team up again on a rescue mission for one of their own.

Chapter Thirty: Mister Corbin

In which our heroes learn the nefarious goings-on inside the house of Robert Carrington's kindly neighbor across the street.

Chapter Thirty-one: The Shard of Nodens

In which our heroes volunteer for an archaeological dig in rural Massachusetts, and discover that a powerful artifact has fallen into the wrong hands.

Chapter Thirty-two: The Code

In which our heroes receive a letter from an old friend containing a mysterious code, which quickly leads to strange and unusual experiences in an old house.
